# 📊 DataQuest Competition – Customer Churn Analysis

## 🧠 Contribution

In this competition, I worked **only on the Data Cleaning & Exploratory Data Analysis (EDA) phase**.

I did **not** build the prediction model or write the executive summary (Pitch section).

---

## 🏢 Scenario

You are hired as a Data Scientist to explain why customers of **XY Company** are leaving.

The task was divided into three main steps:

### 1️⃣ Clean & Explore
- Handle missing values
- Detect and treat outliers
- Perform exploratory data analysis (EDA)
- Generate visualizations
- Understand patterns related to customer churn

### 2️⃣ Predict
Build a machine learning model to predict customer churn.

### 3️⃣ Pitch (The Story)
Write a clear and concise Executive Summary explaining:
- What the model discovered
- Why it matters
- What business decisions should be taken to reduce churn

> Emphasis: Narrative over Numbers  
> A simple model with a strong explanation scores higher than a complex model with no clarity.
